Our Story

Discover the legacy behind LuxeStay and our commitment to excellence

Founded in 2010, LuxeStay began as a vision to redefine luxury hospitality. What started as a single boutique property has now grown into a globally recognized brand, known for its impeccable service and attention to detail.

At LuxeStay, we believe true luxury lies in the details - from the thread count of our linens to the personalized service we provide. Our team undergoes rigorous training to ensure every guest experiences the highest standard of hospitality.

Sustainability is at our core. We've implemented eco-friendly practices throughout our operations, from energy-efficient systems to locally sourced organic amenities, without compromising on luxury.
